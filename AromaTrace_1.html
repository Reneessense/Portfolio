AromaTrace

A paper-based, friction-activated scent printing system

Abstract

AromaTrace is a novel paper-based interface for olfactory composition. By integrating friction-activated microcapsules into paper material, we allow users to physically "draw" or "trace" scents onto a surface. This project explores the potential of scent as a tangible, programmable medium for HCI, moving beyond conventional digital interfaces to engage the sense of smell in creative and communicative ways. It aims to develop a system that democratizes the creation of scent-based media.

Design Process

Our process involved material experimentation with various microencapsulation techniques and paper substrates to optimize scent release via friction. We iterated on the physical interaction model, moving from passive diffusion to an active, gestural input that correlates physical effort with sensory output. The design focuses on simplicity and accessibility, making the "scent pen" and paper feel intuitive.
