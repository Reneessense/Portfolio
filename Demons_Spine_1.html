Demon’s Spine

Fabrication and Technical Specification

Fabrication Process

The `Demon's Spine` was constructed through a multi-stage process involving casting and lighting integration. The goal was to achieve a translucent, bone-like appearance that effectively diffuses the internal light source.

  • Material Testing: Various resins and silicone compounds were tested for optimal light diffusion and structural integrity, resulting in a custom blend that gives the final piece its signature texture.
  • Structural Integration: The lighting and wiring are concealed within the piece, ensuring a seamless, organic look. The base of the sculpture contains the power supply and control unit.

Visual Iterations

Initial prototypes focused on the form, moving from rigid plastic to the final soft-cased material.
